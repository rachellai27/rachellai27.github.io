Hey! I'm Rachel,

a Ph.D. student at Rutgers University studying Computer Science. I'm a member of the PRACSYS Lab, advised by Dr. Kostas Bekris. I'm also a part of an interdisciplinary NSF-funded National Research Traineeship, SOCRATES, which focuses on the integration of robotics with social and behavioral sciences. My research interests are in computer/robot vision and its applications in environmental sustainability.


Prior to this, I attended Boston Univeristy and earned a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science with a minor in Environmental Analysis and Policy.
